Mission Statement

Starbright's goal is to provide financial resources to individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities in order for them to attend Special Needs camps or other social/recreational activities in the community. Starbright also provides emergency funds for our families in need of specific items or services that are generally not funded through other revenue sources.
